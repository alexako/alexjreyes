A 3×4ft wall-mounted living plant frame running on dual ESP32 microcontrollers. One handles grow lighting and Home Assistant integration; the other manages automated watering, humidity via ultrasonic misting, and capacitive moisture sensors calibrated per plant zone. Includes battery backup, a 14-hour light schedule, and Telegram alerts for maintenance. Built on ESPHome firmware.
